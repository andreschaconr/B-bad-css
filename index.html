Breaking Bad.

Synopsis

Breaking Bad is an American television series created and produced by Vince Gilligan. Set and produced in Albuquerque, New Mexico, it consists of the story of Walter White, a chemistry professor who is diagnosed with inoperable lung cancer. Walter goes on to a life of crime producing and distributing methamphetamine alongside Jesse Pinkman.
